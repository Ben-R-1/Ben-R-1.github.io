## Papers

- **Rethinking the Roles of Graphite and Graphene in Lithium‐Ion Batteries From Environmental and Industrial Perspectives**
  [PDF](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/epdf/10.1002/cey2.70099)
  This review paper summarises the most recent efforts from companies and academic researchers to reduce the CO2 emissions from battery grade graphite and graphene production.
  You'd be surprised to see the amount of CO2 currently required to produce these materials, especially considering that Li-ion batteries are meant to be the green energy storage solution of the future.
